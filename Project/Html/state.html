GOA

Goa - the smallest state in India. It lies along coastlines of the Arabian sea. Panaji is the capital of Goa.The area covered is 3,702 square kilometers. It is divided into two Revenue districts, namely North Goa and South Goa. Total population in 2021 is estimated to be 1.59 Million (15.9 Lakhs). In North Goa there are 248 villages and in South Goa is 163. Santa Cruz is largest village. Over a third of part is covered by forest. The state has highest per capita income in India.

SIKKIM

Sikkim is located in the northeastern part of the country, in the eastern Himalayas. Gangtok is the capital of Sikkim. It covers an area of 7096 sq kms (which accounts for 0.2% of the total area of India). The present population of Sikkim stands at 6.79 Lakhs. Total no. of villages are 452. Nathu lal pass is the key geographical feature. India's highest peak lies here - Kangchenjunga. GDP ranking is very poor. It lies second last in GDP ranking in India.

DELHI

Delhi - the national capital of India was built in 20th centuary. It covers an area of 1484 square kilometres of which 783 square kilometres is designated rural and 700 square kilometres is urban. Delhi's 2021 population is now estimated at 31,181,376. It has total of 6,955 villages. It is leading commercial center of India.

RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan - the largest state of India located in north west India. It covers an area of 342,239 square kilometres (132,139 sq mi) or 10.4 per cent of the total geographical area of India. Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan. According to UID, Rajasthan population in 2020 is estimated to be 81 million (8.1 Crores). Total no. of villages are 41,353. Phephana village is the largest village of Rajasthan.

UTTAR PRADESH

Uttar Pradesh - the most populous state and the fourth largest state in India. Its capital is Lucknow, in the west-central part of the state. It covers an area of 93,933 square miles (243,286 square km). Population of Uttar Pradesh state in current year is 241,066,874 (24.10 Crore). With an annual increase of over million people. In 1951, Population of Uttar Pradesh was only 60,274,000. Total no. of villages are 1,07,440. Gahmar is the largest village in India, situated in Uttar Pradesh.

CHATTISGARH

Chattisgarh is in the central part of India. Raipur is the capital of Chattisgarh. Total population estimated is 2.94 Cr. Area Covered is 1,36,034. There are 20,308 villages in total.

KERALA

Kerala - most developed state in India is situated in the southwestern end of the Indian subcontinent. Capital of Kerala is Thiruvananthapuram. Area covered by it is 38,863 square kilometers. Total population is 3,34,06,061. The state with the highest literacy rate in India, is noted for its achievements in education, health, gender equality, social justice, law and order. In addition to these, the state has the lowest infant mortality rate in the country. Total no. of villages are 1,364. Uliyazhathura is the largest village in Kerala.
